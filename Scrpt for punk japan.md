### japanese crust, abridged

Crust is a very wide subgenre of punk. Bands spanning inbetween this genre tend to sound completely different, yet so similar. Common consensus is ; if its metallic and punky in some form or way, its probably gonna sound crusty. Its a very ‘when you hear it’ type of thing.

So how does this correlate to ***japanese crust?**

Well, i forgot to mention a factor, and that is noise.

### NOISE NOISE NOISE ARRAGAHAHGAGAGAG

Noise is probably the most defining characteristic of japanese ‘crasher’ crust. Its the complete opposite of that melodic ‘stadium’ neocrust. Truely embracing the ‘noise not music’ slogan coined by discharge. 

The name “ crasher crust “ was actually coined by members of sds to describe one of glooms preformance! Gloom is commonly known as the first crasher band.
