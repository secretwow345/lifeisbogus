a collection of questions asked to anonymous individuals for research, can be done anywhere.

1 - closed questions ; pre set list of in depth questions for optimal amount of data

2 - unstructured questions ; not scripted interview, but a more in depth qualitive way of questionare

3 - telephone questions ; asking limited yet neccisary questions for opitimal data outcome


----- [[interviews]]

a collection of questions asked to individuals of note relating to the topic at hand, needed to be planned

there are three [[types of interview]] ;

individual or groups

individual ; structured one by one interview

groups ; face to face interview, all being asked the same question

1 - structured interview 
a planned in depth interview, less longer than unstructured due to it being organise thoroughly 

2 - unstructured interview 
unstructured interviews are more in depth and longer than structured, more similar to a conversation than a study

3 - semi structured
a mixture of quantative questions ( planned ) and qualitive questions (unplanned ) in a flexable format
