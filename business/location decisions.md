Location is when the owner of the business chooses an area where business operation is done.

there alot of factors that infuence and relocation decision of a business;

- production methods -  large scale production requires business to be near suppliers 
- market size - being near the customer is important, **especially if the product is heavy or is perishable**
- availability of labour - is there a sufficient supply of labour force in that area? 
- government influence - may offer grants and subsidies to encourange firms to startup over a specific area. **Government helping people work in their land**
- infrastructure and transport - better roads, better bridges for transporting goods to be delivered (e.g. near airport and railway )
- power - sustainable electricity needed
- water and other resources essential for production proccess
