what is triangulation?

triangulation is the mixture and variety of research methods to conclude and verify validity and reliability, this is to come to an unbiased outcome about a single phenomenon.

there are multiple types of triangulation :

1 - methodological triangulation ; the definition of triangulation, taking multiple ways of data collection to come to an outcome that is reliable

2 - data triangulation : taking multiple resources, such as interviewing groups of conflicting ideologies to find a valid outcome

3 - investigator triangulation ; having multiple investigators during research proccess, this has added views and prespectives which can create a more reliable outcome, to reduce individual baises

4 - theory triangulation  ; to find differrent presprectives on the data brought through triangulation. By analyzing findings through different lenses, it increases research credibility, reduces theoretical bias
