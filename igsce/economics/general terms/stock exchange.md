A stock exchange is a centralized, regulated marketplace (physical or virtual) where investors buy and sell shares of publicly traded companies, bonds, and other financial securities

. It acts as a facilitator for capital raising, allowing companies to sell ownership stakes to the public while providing investors with liquidity and transparent, supply-demand-driven pricing.

every country has stock exchange, they exchange or trade money for shares and bonds by the listed companies at said stock exchange.
