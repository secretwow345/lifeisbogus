credit unions are set up by a group of people or group of members of a certain business or society to provide small range financial services ( such as loans ) for the members, people or business like a commercial bank

people lending money to eachother, lending for the people, not for profit

examples : 
- cooperative society and cooperative banking ( financial help for members in case of emergencies , mutual aid )
- housing federation society ( providing money for housing )
- microfinancing services ( small funds )
