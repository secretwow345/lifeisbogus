#travel-and-tourism

[[what is tourism]]?

tourism is the temporary short term movement of people at a destinations outside places where they usually live and work

"tourism" defined by the united nations world tourism organization (UNWTO) : Tourism comprises the activities of people travelling to and staying in places outside their usual environment for not more than one consecutive year for leasure, business and other purposes

[[how many types of tourism]]?

there are three types of tourism ;

- domestic tourism
- inbound tourism
- outbound tourism

what is [[domestic tourism]]?

Travelling in between  within their own country, usually in holidays, short breaks, and day trips like events

what is [[outbound tourism]]?

when people leave or travel away from their home country to other countries for leasure time or visiting family

**outbound = travelling OUT of the home country to visit other countries**

what is [[inbound tourism]]?

when people enter a country for holiday or other purposes

**inbound = travelling IN a new country seperete from your home country**
