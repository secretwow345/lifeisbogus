#travel-and-tourism

[[what is tourism]]?

tourism is the temporary short term movement of people at a destinations outside places where they usually live and work

"tourism" defined by the united nations world tourism organization (UNWTO) : Tourism comprises the activities of people travelling to and staying in places outside their usual environment for not more than one consecutive year for leasure, business and other purposes

[[how many types of tourism]]?

- domestic tourism
- inbound tourism
- outbound tourism
